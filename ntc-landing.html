The Director

"The best content
is made by people who care
about the frame."

Jadon Cal Fitzpatrick is a filmmaker, director, and the founder of New Terrain Creative. His work has screened at Tribeca, Telluride, and Gasparilla — where his debut feature took home the Grand Jury Award. He builds NTC on that same foundation: cinematic intention, technical depth, and a story-first philosophy applied to every project, regardless of length or format.

NTC operates between Los Angeles and Tampa with a full production kit, in-house post, and a lean crew structure built for premium output without overhead bloat.

I
Concept & Story

Every project starts with what it's trying to say. We build story structure, interview frameworks, and creative approaches before a camera turns on — so the shoot has a reason for every shot.

II
Production

Director-led field production with full ownership of camera, lighting, and audio. The eye behind the lens is the same one that built the story plan — no handoffs, no gaps in vision.

III
Post & Delivery

In-house editing, color grading, audio finishing, and multi-format delivery. The project isn't done until every deliverable looks like the vision we agreed on at the start.
